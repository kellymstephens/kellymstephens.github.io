Reading with Dragons: A Young-Adult Book Blog

I have created and am currently maintaining a blog called Reading with Dragons: Musings about YA Books, Heroines, and Culture. As an avid reader who loves media, books, and good conversation, I use my site to create a warm community, write book reviews, and publish articles about news and trends in young-adult fiction, especially fantasy and science-fiction.

While I post about a variety of topics, I particularly like to focus on books that have strong, well-rounded heroines, whom I have begun to feature in my new Kick-Butt Heroines profiles. I have also started to create mini-blog series that come from The Dragon's Hoard. This is where I post in-depth book reviews that explore a book's character arcs, world-building, and other unique aspects. It is also where I post fun lists and playlists.
