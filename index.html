Language and Technology: A Love Story

It’s a bit of an ironic story, really. If you had asked me as a child what I wanted to do when I grew up, writing and technology would not have been at the top of my list. In fact, I found reading in general to be difficult, but that was mostly because I had yet to find the right book. Like so many other children, my love for reading and language was sparked by J.K. Rowling’s Harry Potter books. Since then, I have become a voracious reader and writer, and my greatest passion is working with words, whether I’m working to create a story, communicate a message, or build a community online through blogging and social media.

I am incredibly excited to graduate from the University of Florida this May 2015 with majors in English and History, and a minor in Educational Studies. Once I graduate, I hope to start the next chapter of my life by transferring my writing skills and my passion for language, people, and technology into a career with social media and communication. Here you will be able to learn more about me: my writing, my work experience, and the causes I am passionate about. For more information about my background, please head over to my About page.
